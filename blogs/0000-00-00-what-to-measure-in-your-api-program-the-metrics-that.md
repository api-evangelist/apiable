The article argues most API teams focus on infrastructure metrics like uptime and latency while leadership cares about business outcomes, outlining four key categories: partner adoption (time to first call, active partners), revenue and monetization (revenue per partner, usage vs. billing accuracy), program health (signup trends, endpoint popularity), and onboarding effectiveness (drop-off points, support tickets). It emphasizes connecting usage data with partner identity to turn an API program from a cost center into a revenue channel.
