Every third-party application connecting to FHIR APIs must be registered: an OAuth client created, scopes assigned, credentials issued, and the registration recorded, whether done manually or automated with Dynamic Client Registration (DCR). The article argues the difference is cost, and that neither manual nor fully automated approaches alone provide adequate governance, since organizations need both speed and compliance oversight.
